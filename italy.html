Italy is a country known for its rich history, art, architecture, and culture. From the ancient ruins of Rome to the romantic canals of Venice, Italy offers a unique experience that feels like a journey through time. It is also famous for its world-renowned cuisine such as pizza, pasta, gelato, and espresso. Italian culture places great importance on family, traditions, and enjoying life slowly, especially through food and social gatherings. Traveling in Italy feels like exploring an open-air museum filled with beauty, flavor, and history.
